Case Study — Physiotherapy

The Dance Clinic.

Branding and web design for Wollongong's physiotherapy hub for dancers — an identity as considered as the care.

ServicesBranding · Web Design · Identity
SectorPhysiotherapy / Allied Health
EngagementProject

01 / The brief

A practice for dancers, with no identity to match the care.

A specialist physio clinic for dancers needed an identity and website that felt as precise and caring as their practice — and spoke to a specific community.

A specialist practice deserves an identity that is just as specialist.

02 / What we did

An identity built around the patient, not the template.

We shaped a brand for the dance community, then built a web experience that carries it from first visit to booking.

01

A distinctive brand identity

A considered visual language that sets the clinic apart in allied health — precise, warm and unmistakably its own.

02

Tone of voice for the dance community

Language written to speak to dancers directly — fluent in their world, never generic clinical copy.

03

A web experience built around the patient

A site structured around how a dancer actually decides on care, shaped to feel as considered as the practice itself.

04

Clear paths to book

Booking made obvious and effortless throughout the site, turning interest into a confirmed appointment.

03 / The work

A look at what we built.

Brand identity, website and design — built to set the clinic apart and make booking effortless.

04 / The result

A brand that stands apart, and a site that books.

An identity that holds its own in allied health, and a web experience that turns visitors into bookings.

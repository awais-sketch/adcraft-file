Case Study — Healthcare Platform

WebDoctor.

A secure, trusted platform for an Australian online-doctor service — built to convert patients while protecting their data.

ServicesWeb Design · Platform · UX
SectorOnline Healthcare / Telehealth
EngagementProject

01 / The brief

Patients have to trust it within seconds.

An online doctor service has to earn confidence the moment a patient lands — and the platform behind it has to stay secure and reliable while it does.

Online care lives or dies on trust built in seconds.

02 / What we did

A reassuring journey, on a platform built to be trusted.

We designed the experience around how patients decide to seek care online — leading with trust, and backing it with a secure, reliable platform.

01

A clear, reassuring patient journey

A calm path from landing to consultation, shaped around how patients actually choose online care.

02

Trust cues and credentials presented early

The signals that build confidence — credentials, reassurance, clarity — placed where patients see them first.

03

A secure, reliable platform experience

Built to protect patient data and to perform, so the experience feels safe at every step.

04

Compliant messaging throughout

Every line written to stay within the guidelines for advertising a regulated health service.

03 / The work

A look at what we built.

Platform interface, feature screens and brand work — designed to build trust and convert patients online.

04 / The result

A secure platform patients are ready to trust.

A successful launch with a smoother path from landing to consultation — built honestly, with security at its core.
